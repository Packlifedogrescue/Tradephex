The Rules

Five rules.
Read once.

01 · Profit Target
Hit 6% net profit.
Reach a six percent gain on your starting balance to pass. Counted on closed trades only. No partial credit, no rounding.
02 · Maximum Drawdown
Stay above 5% account drawdown.
Trailing or static depending on intraday versus EOD. Breach this, the evaluation ends. The platform enforces it in real time.
03 · Daily Loss Limit
Don't lose more than 2% in a session.
Counted from session open. Hit two percent down for the day, the platform locks you out until the next session opens.
04 · Minimum Trading Days
Trade at least 5 days.
Five separate trading days where you place at least one trade. No weekend or holiday days count. No maximum cap on calendar time.
05 · Consistency Rule
No single day exceeds 40% of total profit.
If your single best day is more than forty percent of your overall gain, you'll need to keep trading to balance out. Prevents lottery wins.
06 · Profit Split
Keep 90% of every dollar.
After passing, you keep ninety percent of net profits on your funded account. Flat. Same on payout one or payout one hundred.
