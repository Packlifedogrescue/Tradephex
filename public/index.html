Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Is this real money?+
No. Tradephex evaluations use simulated market data. Payouts to funded traders come from the evaluation fee pool, not from live market trading profits.
How long do I have to pass?+
No time limit. The only requirement is a minimum of 5 trading days. Your monthly subscription keeps your eval active for as long as you need. Take your time and trade your process.
What happens if I blow the eval?+
Purchase a reset at a reduced fee or buy a fresh evaluation. No penalty beyond the fee. Your account is not failed — only the evaluation attempt ends.
When do I get paid?+
Submit a payout request from your dashboard at any time after passing. We process within 5 business days via PayPal, bank ACH, or crypto.
Do I need to download anything?+
No. Tradephex runs entirely in your web browser. No downloads, no installations, no plugins. Works on any device.
What instruments can I trade?+
ES, NQ, YM, RTY, MES, MNQ, GC, SI, CL, NG, ZC, ZW, ZS, ZN, ZB and more. All 22 CME futures contracts are available on the platform.
