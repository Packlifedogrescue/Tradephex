Refund Policy
Effective: April 24, 2026

Summary: All Tradephex purchases are final. We evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis in exceptional circumstances. Contact support@tradephex.com within 7 days of purchase if you have a concern.

1. General Policy

All sales made through the Tradephex Platform are considered final. Tradephex operates a no-refund policy as our default position for all digital products, subscription services, and evaluation programs.

2. Memberships & Subscriptions

Monthly and yearly membership subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing period has begun. When you cancel a subscription:

  • Future billing is stopped immediately
  • You retain access to the Platform until the end of your current paid billing period
  • No partial or pro-rated refunds are issued for unused time
  • Annual subscriptions are non-refundable once purchased
3. Evaluation Programs

Evaluation program fees are non-refundable once access to the evaluation environment has been granted. This applies regardless of whether:

  • The evaluation has been started or not
  • The evaluation has been passed or failed
  • You decide to stop trading before completing the evaluation

You may be eligible to retry a failed evaluation by purchasing a new evaluation program at the standard fee.

4. Digital Products

Digital products, downloadable content, and any product that has been accessed or delivered are non-refundable once accessed.

5. Case-by-Case Exceptions

Refunds may be issued at the sole discretion of Tradephex LLC in rare and exceptional circumstances. To request consideration of a refund:

  • Contact support@tradephex.com within 7 days of your purchase
  • Include your order details and a clear explanation of your request
  • Our team will review your request and respond within 5 business days

Approval of refund requests is not guaranteed and is entirely at our discretion.

6. Auto-Renewal

All subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle. It is your responsibility to cancel your subscription before the renewal date if you do not wish to be charged for the next period. Renewal charges processed are not eligible for refunds.

7. Chargeback Policy

Filing a chargeback or payment dispute without first contacting our support team constitutes a breach of our Terms of Service. This will result in immediate and permanent account termination.

By purchasing any Tradephex product, you agree to contact support@tradephex.com before filing any dispute with your payment provider. Tradephex reserves the right to submit evidence to contest any chargeback and to pursue recovery of disputed funds through available legal channels.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Refund Policy or a specific transaction, please contact us at support@tradephex.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 business days.
