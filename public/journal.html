⚡ The Consistency Rule
No single trade can account for more than the allowed percentage of your total profit. This rule exists to ensure you're trading consistently — not getting lucky on one big trade. We fund disciplined traders, not gamblers.
Example: On a $50K account with $3,000 total profit, no single trade can exceed $1,200 (40%)
