Intraday vs EOD — What's the Difference?
Choose the eval type that matches your trading style.
⚡ INTRADAY EVAL
Drawdown is tracked in real-time including open trades. If your account equity dips below the 4% limit even for one second — including unrealized losses — the rule is breached immediately.
Best for: Scalpers and day traders who close positions quickly and manage risk intraday.
$50K: $79/mo  ·  $100K: $149/mo  ·  $150K: $199/mo
🌙 EOD EVAL
Drawdown is calculated only on your closed P&L at 4PM ET. Open trade fluctuations do not matter unless they are realized by the close. You can hold through intraday swings without fear.
Best for: Traders who hold through pullbacks or have wider stops and need breathing room intraday.
$50K: $149/mo  ·  $100K: $249/mo  ·  $150K: $349/mo
📌
Both Evaluation types become EOD Funded Accounts. Regardless of which Evaluation type you pass, your Funded Account uses EOD drawdown rules. Open trades do not count against your drawdown until the 4:00 PM ET close.
⚠️
Violation = Evaluation Closed. Hitting your max drawdown or daily loss limit will immediately close your evaluation. A reset fee applies to start a new evaluation on the same account size.

The 40% Consistency Rule — Explained
Here's exactly how it works with a real example.
📘
Example: You've made $5,000 total profit and want to request a payout.

Your best single day was $1,800.
40% of $5,000 = $2,000.
$1,800 is under $2,000 — you pass the consistency check.

If your best day was $2,500 — that's 50% of your total profit — the payout would be blocked until you've made more profit on other days to bring that percentage down below 40%.

Frequently Asked Questions
Can't find your answer? Email us at support@tradephex.com
What happens if I fail my evaluation?
If you hit your max drawdown or daily loss limit, your evaluation closes. You can restart with a reset fee — which is lower than the original subscription price. Your account size and rules stay the same.
Is there a time limit on the evaluation?
No. Your monthly subscription keeps your evaluation active for as long as you're subscribed. Take the time you need — there's no deadline.
Can I trade news events?
Yes. There are no restrictions on when you can trade. You can hold through news events, trade during NFP, FOMC — whatever your strategy calls for.
Can I use automated trading or EAs?
Yes, automated strategies are allowed. However, account sharing or using the same strategy across multiple accounts is not permitted and will result in account termination.
Can I hold trades overnight or over the weekend?
No. All positions must be closed by 4:00 PM ET each trading day. Any open positions at the close will count against your EOD drawdown calculation. Overnight and weekend holds are not permitted on either Evaluation or Funded accounts.
What markets can I trade?
The Tradephex platform supports ES/MES, NQ/MNQ, YM/MYM, Crude Oil (CL), Gold (GC), and more. Full instrument list is available inside the platform.
When does the consistency rule kick in?
The 40% consistency rule only applies to funded accounts when requesting a payout. There is no consistency rule during the evaluation phase.
How do I request a payout?
Once you're funded, go to your dashboard → My Evaluation → Request Payout. Enter the amount, select your payment method, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email and payment within 5 business days.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. If you cancel mid-month, your evaluation remains active until the end of your billing period. Cancelling closes your evaluation — it cannot be resumed after cancellation.
